Frequently Asked Questions/अक्सर पूछे जाने वाले प्रश्न

What is Sevaa Bandhu?

Sevaa Bandhu is a platform that connects workers and clients, making it easy to find and hire skilled labor for local tasks.

Who can use Sevaa Bandhu?

Both workers looking for jobs and clients needing workers for tasks can use this platform.

How can I register as a worker?

Visit the "Register as Worker" section on the website, fill out your details, and submit the form.

Is Sevaa Bandhu free to use?

Registration is free for both workers and clients.You can currently use this at free of cost.

How can I hire a worker?

Browse the "Categories" section, search for workers based on your requirements, and contact them directly.

Can I hire multiple workers for a project?

Yes, you can hire as many workers as needed for your project.

Are workers verified on Sevaa Bandhu?

Yes, we verify workers Id Proof documents before listing them with us on Sevaa Bandhu.
